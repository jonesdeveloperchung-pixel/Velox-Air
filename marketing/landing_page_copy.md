## [FAQ]

**Q: Does it work on my 2013 iPad Air?**
A: Yes! Velox Air uses a lightweight Canvas 2D renderer specifically designed for legacy browsers.

**Q: Do I need to install an app on the tablet?**
A: No. Just scan the QR code to open the web client. You can tap "Add to Home Screen" for a fullscreen app experience.

**Q: Can I use it for gaming?**
A: No. For gaming, check out **Velox Warp** (Pro Tier). Velox Air is designed for passive reading and monitoring.
